Foreword from McGuire Law

Dear fellow citizens,

For over 30 years, McGuire Law has proudly served South Carolina with one mission: to defend and protect the rights of every individual who faces law enforcement.

"Remain Silent" reflects that mission. This app is designed to empower you to stand firm, stay calm, and exercise your rights with confidence.

Every day, we meet people who say, "I didn't know what to do," or "I thought cooperating would help." The truth is, you should never speak to law enforcement without an attorney present. We must assert our rights to protect ourselves and our legal system.

Whether you are a lifelong South Carolinian or a recent arrival, your rights under the U.S. and South Carolina Constitutions are the same. And these rights matter.

You'll find practical information in this app, clearly and respectfully explained. We've built this so that anyone, regardless of education, background, or citizenship, can understand what to do in stressful situations. The right to remain silent, the right to refuse a search, and the right to legal counsel are not just abstract legal concepts. These rights are tools for protecting your freedom.

At McGuire Law, we believe education is the most vigorous defense. Keep this app on your desktop, read it carefully, and share it with your family and friends. You never know when understanding your rights could make all the difference.

If you or someone you love is facing questioning, detention, or arrest, call McGuire Law immediately. The call is always free, and we are always available.

Stay safe,
